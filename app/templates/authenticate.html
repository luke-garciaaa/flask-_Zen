How to Authorize the use of your Spotify Account



In order to access your personalized insights, you use give this application permission to access your listening history and top songs. You will be redirected to a secured page by Spotify that will enable you to give us authorization. Don't worry, we don't sell any of your information :)

Authorize your Spotify Account here.

If you haven't authorized the use of your Spotify Account, you will be redirected to a Spotify-focused page that will ask you to authorize this app to access your listening history, top songs, and top artists. We require this authorization in order to access your insights, but don't worry, this will only give us temporary access, and you can revoke access at any time.

Will this put my personal information in jeopardy?

At Spotify InSite, we value our users' privacy first and foremost. You can control which applications have access to your personal data, and our temporary access to your information can be revoked at any time. We recommend guarding your login information very closely, and if you have any issues concerning privacy issues, please contact site administration.
