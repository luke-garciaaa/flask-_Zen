Mission Statement



Our mission is to help provide users with personalized listening insights in order to help you learn more about the music you already love.

Why music?

As an avid music listener, our developer wanted to spread Spotify's music insights to everyone who wants to see them.

How was this project developed?

Our project leverages the Spotify Application Programming Interface (API) in order to retrive your personalized insights. This program was implemented using Flask, Python, CSS, HTML, and Bootstrap.

User Guide



In order to use this website, our users will have to follow a couple easy steps in order to retrieve their insights.

Step 1: Creating an Account / Logging In.

If you've already made a Spotify InSite account, please navigate to the login page.
If you haven't made a Spotify InSite account, please navigate to the register page. These pages will require your email address, along with a password.

Step 2: Authorizing your Spotify Account.

After you've created an account and logged in, please navigate to the "Authenticate Spotify" page. This page will give further information on how we use the Spotify API to access your personalized listening data.

Step 3: Accessing your personalized listening insights.

After authorizing the use of your Spotify Account, you will automatically be naviagated to the "Manage Track Insights" page. In order to access your track insights, click the 'Add Insight' button, and you will be able to view your insights in a table format. In order to view your album insights, repeat this process on the "Manage Album Insights" page.
